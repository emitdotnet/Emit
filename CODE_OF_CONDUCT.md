# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project
- Show empathy toward others

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/emitdotnet/Emit/issues). The maintainer will review and take appropriate action, which may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community.

All reports will be reviewed fairly. The maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the reporter.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels associated with this project.
